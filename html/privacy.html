MedTrack Privacy and Data Policy

MedTrack is committed to protecting your personal information and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This policy explains how we collect, process, store, and protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you provide voluntarily during registration or when using our services. This includes:

  • Name
  • NHS number
  • Date of Birth
  • Address
  • Email & Contact details

2. Purpose of Processing

We use your personak information to:

  • Manage appointments and provide healthcare services
  • Maintain accurate medical records
  • Communicate important updates regarding your care
  • Ensure patient safety and service quality

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

MedTrack processes persnonal data based on:

  • Your explicit consent
  • The necessity to provide healthcare services
  • Compliance with legal obligations

4. Data Storage & Retention

Your personal data is securely stored for the duration of your active account. After account closure, data is retained only as required by medical record-keeping regulations, after which it is securely deleted.

5. Data Sharing

Your information is shared only with authorized medical professionals within our organization. It may also be shared with external service providers when necessary to deliver healthcare services, under strict confidentiality agreements. Your data will not be sold, shared for marketing purposes, or disclosed to third parties without your explicit consent.

6. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Strict access controls
  • Regular security audits & monitoring

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability
To exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at +230 403 5500.

8. Automated Decision-Making/profiling

MedTrack does not use automated decision-making or profiling that significantly affects your rights.
